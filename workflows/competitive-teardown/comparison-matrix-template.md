## pattern-library.md

One entry per named pattern.

### [Pattern name]
- What it is: one or two sentences.
- Who uses it: competitors, with evidence files.
- Variations worth noting.

## gaps.md

One entry per gap.

### [Gap]
- What no competitor does well, with the evidence that suggests it.
- Why it might be an opportunity — and the obvious reason it might be a graveyard.
- What we would need to learn before betting on it (the human decision this feeds).
