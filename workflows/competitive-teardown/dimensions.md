# Teardown dimensions (template)

Every competitor folder must contain evidence for each dimension, or a note explaining why it could not be collected (paywall, sales-gated, region-locked). Edit this file per teardown — keep only the dimensions the design decision needs.

## ONBOARDING
- Ordered screenshots from signup to first meaningful action.
- Step count, required fields, whether account creation can be deferred.
- Time from signup to an empty state or template choice.

## PRICING
- Full-page screenshot of the pricing page.
- Tiers, prices, billing toggle, what is gated at each tier, trial terms.
- The in-product upgrade flow if reachable on a trial account.

## POSITIONING
- Homepage headline and subhead, verbatim, with URL.
- The three claims the marketing leans on hardest (verbatim, with URLs).

## CORE FLOWS
- Screenshots of: [the two or three flows that matter to your decision, e.g. creating the first object, inviting a teammate, the main working view].

## CLAIM CROSS-CHECK
- For each positioning claim: what the walkthrough showed (supports, contradicts, could not verify), citing the evidence file.
