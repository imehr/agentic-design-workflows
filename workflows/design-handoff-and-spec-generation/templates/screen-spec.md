## Interaction states

For each interactive element: default, hover, focus, active, disabled, loading, error, empty.
Mark any state not shown in the design as **Open question** — do not invent it.

## Accessibility annotations

- Roles and accessible names for every control.
- Focus order and what receives focus on entry, error, and success.
- What is announced on async changes (role=status / role=alert).
- Contrast notes for any text or control on a non-default surface.

## Edge cases

Long content, no content, slow network, permission and error variants, localization length.

## Acceptance criteria

Testable statements. Each one must be checkable without asking the designer.

## Open questions

Anything the design does not answer. Each question gets an owner during the answer session.
