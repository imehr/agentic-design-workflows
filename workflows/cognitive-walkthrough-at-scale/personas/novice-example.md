# Persona: Novice administrator (example — copy and adapt)

A walkthrough persona is a knowledge profile, not a poster. State what this person knows, what they have already done, and which conventions they can be assumed to recognize. Leave out demographics the four questions never ask about.

## Summary

Recently given admin rights for their team's workspace. Comfortable with everyday office software, has never administered this product or any comparable one, and does not know the product's internal vocabulary.

## Knows

- General web conventions: forms, menus, search, undo.
- Their own organization's team structure and who needs access to what.
- That they were sent an invite email and have successfully signed in once.

## Does not know

- The product's terminology (workspaces, roles, baselines, policies — whatever your product calls things).
- Where settings live or that some settings are per-user while others are per-workspace.
- Keyboard shortcuts, swipe gestures, or any convention learned only through prior use of this product.

## Already done before the first task

- Accepted the invite and signed in.
- Nothing else: no setup wizard completed, no documentation read.
